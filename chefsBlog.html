Chef's Blog


Our chefs are passionated by food. They get inspired by our traditional cuisine and enjoy recreating dishes creating an unique flavour.
Here they share some secrets with you so you can recreate it in your own kitchen.


A Steak to
dream about

A delicious and suculent steak doesn't have to be complicated. The secret is to keep simple. Rubbing a sting of olive oil, ground salt and pepper on both sides are all the condiments your steak needs. Take this beauty to a frying pan and until if gets a nice tan and voila.

The zest your
fish needs

Add flavour to you fish can be made nice and easy. The good and old squeeze of lemon or lime juice can give freshness to you dish.

(Even)easier
porridge for
rushed mornings

Breakfast is important, we know that. But rushed mornings can ruin your diet and make you skip this important meal. To avoid that prepare you porridge on the night before. But how? In a glass bowl, just add in layers the oats, a fuit of your choice (bananas, blueberries, strawberries), milk, and add peanut butter, honey, cinnamon, according to your taste. The oats will be soaked in the next morning and you can enjoy your brekkie as you wish.
